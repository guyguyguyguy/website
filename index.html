BigaBid is incredibly especially extremely smart and innovative.

Founded by industry veterans and entrepreneurs, BigaBid is a data driven and data science focused solution designed for performance marketers in the mobile advertising industry. BigaBid is currently developing a bidder application based on cluster statistics and the “Personal Collage” concept. The application will allow the company to perform accurate, non-cookie based, algorithmic media buying throughout the different mobile ad exchanges, with the goal of reaching a positive ROI on every single ad campaign.


BigaBid's Brain

Our unique and proprietary methods lie in the scientific models that determine which group a certain user belongs to and automatically matches them to a relevant, available offer. Since we believe machine data analysis and mathematics cannot compete with the human brain’s ability to deduce and induce, we pair the mathematical, machine driven conclusions with a psychological algorithm, based on the “Personal Collage” concept, by which every person is unique, however the different characteristics are based on predetermined, well known and studied types. Using powerful machine learning algorithms we are building a neural network kind of “brain” for our bidder, mimicking the human decision making mechanisms in different aspects.

Every single ad impression bid makes us better, smarter and closer to our goal. Every such impression allows our bidder to gain more insight. While there’s never a second chance to make a first impression, for BigaBid, even the first impression counts – no matter how bad it is.

The BigaBid Brain is a feat of psychological analysis algorithms combined with cluster statistics algorithms. The unique and proprietary mechanism was built with the “Personal Collage” theory; psychological profiling and sophisticated data clustering, all in an RTB environment. In summary, a machine learning system that utilizes mathematical logic with that of a psychologist, in an attempt to mimic a human’s mind, decision making and personal profiling mechanisms.
